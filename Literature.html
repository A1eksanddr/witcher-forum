ANDRZEJ SAPKOWSKI

(born June 21, 1948) is a Polish science fiction writer and publicist, author of the popular fantasy saga THE WITCHER.

An economist by education, he worked as a sales manager, then began translating fantasy literature at the age of 42. He wrote his first story, The Witcher, to participate in a competition organized by the Polish magazine Fantastica, and Sapkowski's story came in third place!
